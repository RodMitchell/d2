There are no lectures or computer labs on legal holidays or when Robarts Library is closed. Course durations that include legal holidays will have additional time assigned to make up for lost days.

All courses consist of instructor-led training from 9am to noon and a computer lab session from 1pm to 4pm. Our computer labs are only available at the times specified. Where possible we will make remote access available for completing computer lab work from home or other remote locations.
